About D'Mexican

I have spent my life thus far traveling the world, East to West, North and South, I have eaten at restraints on Five Continents, enjoying exotic food, flavors and memorable experiences. D'Mexican a little hole is the wall in a strip mall, one building off according to Google Maps, is hands down the best food I can remember. The vegetables were noticeably fresh, every aspect of our dishes was remarkable. best we had was the potable mushroom fajitas. This place is a two and a half hour drive from my home,but worth the trip.
